Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a subfield of AI that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language. It involves the use of computational techniques to analyze, understand, and generate human language.

One of the common applications of NLP is Sentiment Analysis, where we determine the sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral) of a piece of text.
