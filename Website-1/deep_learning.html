Deep Learning

Deep Learning is a subset of machine learning that utilizes artificial neural networks to model and solve complex problems. These neural networks consist of multiple layers of interconnected nodes, enabling them to learn hierarchical representations of data.

One of the popular applications of deep learning is image classification, where deep neural networks can classify images into different categories.
